Precaution

STD

Some STDs, like HPV and hepatitis B, can be prevented through vaccination. If you are sexually active, getting regular STD screenings can help with early detection and treatment if you are infected, and can also prevent further transmission. Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis (PrEP) if you are at high risk for HIV, PrEP medication can be prescribed by a healthcare provider to reduce the risk of HIV transmission.

Precaution

Safe sex

If you are sexually active, consistent and correct use of latex or polyurethane condoms can significantly reduce the risk of STIs and prevent unintended pregnancies. If you are not ready for or do not want children, using a reliable form of birth control, in addition to condoms for STI protection, can be essential.

symptoms

UTI

A common and uncomfortable symptom is a burning sensation when you urinate. Urge to urinate more often than usual. In some cases, UTIs may cause discomfort in the pelvic region. In severe cases if the infection has progressed to the kidneys, you might experience a fever and chills.
