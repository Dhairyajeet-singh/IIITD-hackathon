About Us

Our Team ctrl_alt_del have been working on the project Healthy-HER which revolves around women and teens health and helping them contact doctors, gynecologists, psychocologists, providing healthcare services in remote areas. Visiting a gynecologist, a psychocologist is still a taboo subject even in this modern world. Hence we provide a platform where women can connect users with expert professionals.

Our Idea

Our idea revolves around the development of a platform which would include the following key features:

  • 1) Easy access to gynecologists
  • 2) Nearest hospitals and medical clinics according to needs
  • 3) Detecting medical issues by entering symptoms of the patient and checking if a visit to the doctor is necessary.
  • 4) Blogs which would educate people especially teenagers about STDs, UTIs and Teenage pregnancies over women health all over the world

Our Goal

Our Goal is to provide health related services to women by keeping their identity anonymous in both the rural areas and urban areas and easy access to doctors and hospitals in remote areas in remote areas.
